Purple Hats Project works to hand-craft purple hats for the organization Prevent Child Abuse New Jersey. This organization delivers these hats to hospitals where they are handed out to new parents to raise awareness of Shaken Baby Syndrome. For more information visit: https://honorscollege.rutgers.edu/features/mira-yin-sarah-velez-why-we-give
